Project Details

🌊 Ocean Wave & Sea Scene

  • Realistic water simulation using Blender's fluid dynamics engine
  • Dynamic wave generation with proper wave physics and foam simulation
  • Advanced lighting setup for underwater and surface effects
  • Procedural texturing for ocean bed and underwater rocks
  • Realistic material properties for water refraction and reflection
  • Environmental lighting with atmospheric perspective

🌧️ Rain Particle System

  • Advanced particle effect system for realistic rain simulation
  • Multiple particle layers for depth and volume
  • Physics-based raindrops with gravity and collision detection
  • Volumetric lighting for rain visibility in various lighting conditions
  • Wind simulation affecting particle trajectory and distribution
  • Real-time performance optimization for efficient rendering

  • Realistic ship materials including wood, metal, and weathered surfaces

  • Advanced rigging for potential ship animation
  • Accurate naval architecture and proportions
  • Professional lighting setup for maritime scenes

🚀 Space Scene

  • Planetary modeling with realistic surface details and atmospheres

  • Advanced material creation for planetary surfaces (rock, ice, gas)
