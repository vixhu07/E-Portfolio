Project Overview

Created an interactive 360-degree panoramic walkthrough system that allows users to explore Chandigarh University campus with intuitive point-and-teleport navigation. This innovative experience enables seamless campus exploration from anywhere in the world.

The project was showcased and presented to the Hon. Chancellor Sir at the Corporate Advisory Board (CAB) meeting in 2025, demonstrating advanced immersive technology capabilities in educational environments.
