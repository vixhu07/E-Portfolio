Project Overview

Worked with a team to create a comprehensive 3D simulation of Chandigarh University's campus with full spatial navigation and VR integration. The project demonstrated proficiency in 3D environment design, optimization, and multi-platform deployment.

The project was showcased at the Project Expo 2025 (2nd Prize Winner), presented to the Hon. Chancellor Sir at the Corporate Advisory Board (CAB) meeting, and deployed on both PC and VR platforms (Meta Quest 3 and Meta Quest 2).
